Project #4 Exquisite Corpse

Description

The sketch above is a combination of three sketches to produce an exquisite corpse named St. Jean-Pierre de Florida. Our friend here has lived many lives before being reincarnated as an angel - looking out for everyone around. Before being reincarnated into an angel, they were once a gay Republican, a French fisherman and a little black girl. After years of living on the human plane, St. Jean-Pierre finally ascended into their celestial form and became an angel. St. Jean-Pierre now guides people he comes across through the difficulties in life.

Design Process

I started by analysing the corpses provided by my partners to understand the feasibility of my own final corpse. After determining the body parts I would use, I proceeded to sketch a rough idea on paper to visualise the code I'd develop on P5JS. I then experimented with functions and ended up making the clouds move to showcase St. Jean-Pierre's capability to soar through the sky.
