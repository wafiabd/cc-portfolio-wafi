Project #3 Experimental Clock

Description

For Assignment 3, I had to envision an experimental clock that recorded time in an unconventional way. I referred to Xin Xin's code and tried to play around with the examples available from Critical Computation Lecture and Lab sessions.

Design Process

I started by sketching a draft in Figma to experiment with unconventional methods of tracking time. While the process was a little tricky, I was able to come up with some ideas that captured different the idea of tracking time in unconventional ways. While coding, I realised that it was difficult to develop the ideas I had in mind and had to experiment with what was available to me, skillset-wise. Due to my limitations, I used code that I was already familiar with instead.

Reflection

I've discovered that I wasn't able to translate my ideas into code due to technical limitations. This was frustrating because I wasn't able to develop and capture the essence of the ideas I developed in Figma. This assignment has definitely challenged my perspective in terms of designing and creative coding. I plan to revisit this assignment and improve on my clock.
